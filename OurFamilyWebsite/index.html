Welcome to this platform

This website is a special home for all the sons, daughters,sons-in-law, daughters-in-law and the grand children of the Tibanyendera family.


At the heart of our family stands Tibanyendera Deogratious, whose legacy of love, strength, and guidance continues to inspire us every day.

Objectives of creating this Platform

To ease access to stories, photos, and connections that define who we are, from our beloved parents and their ten children, to the wonderful spouses who joined our family, and the precious grandchildren who carry on this family's name.


To help us celebrate our roots, preserve our history, and strengthen the bond between every member of our growing family. Whether you are near or far, this is your space to reconnect, remember, and belong.
